About Us

Welcome to Saint Letha's Fruit Sando, a charming cafe inspired by the beloved Japanese fruit sando, where the tradition of soft, fluffy bread meets the sweetness of seasonal fruit. The fruit sando, a sandwich made of fresh fruit and whipped cream, originated in Japan in the mid-20th century, gaining popularity for its simplicity and luxurious taste. Initially a treat for special occasions, it quickly became a staple in Japan’s cafes and bakeries.

At Saint Letha's Fruit Sando, we’ve embraced this iconic dish, blending the classic with a modern twist. Each sandwich is carefully crafted using only the finest ingredients, from ripe, locally sourced fruits to our signature airy, pillowy bread. Our cafe offers a nostalgic yet contemporary experience, celebrating both the rich history of the fruit sando and its role in Japan’s café culture. Come indulge in a bite of sweetness, and discover how a humble sandwich can tell a story of craftsmanship, culture, and joy.

Join us for a cozy atmosphere, friendly service, and a taste of something special.
